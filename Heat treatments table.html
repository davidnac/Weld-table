General Notes

  • 1. Air cool - liquid quench not allowed.
  • 2. Air cool in Inert gas & Vaccum - parts shall be cooled to 1000ºF (538ºC) by back filing with forced argon or nitrogen. Cooling rates 17-25ºC/min to 538ºC.
  • 3. Air cool in Air Furnace - air cool or faster means the part shall be taken out of the furnace and cool in the air environment. Fans may be used to aid cooling. Cooling rates 11-17ºC/min to 538ºC.
